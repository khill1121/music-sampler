Half an Orange: "Metade de Laranja"

Special Permission for Use from Andrew Spellman

The musical duo Half an Orange is comprised of two Ohio natives: Andrew Spellman and Michael Maloof. The name Half an Orange comes from the Portuguese expression "Metade de Laranja." The expression is supposed to refer to how everyone is half of an orange looking for what completes them, aka other half of the orange. Michael learned this term while living on a beach in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil for a year. The groups lyrics and songs aim to reflect this journey to find themselves and what completes them. The theme of the music this duo creates is what personally draws me in. I recommend this music to anyone who feels like they're seaching for who they want to become. It doesn't hurt if you're a little stuck in your memories of the good old days too.

Enjoy!
